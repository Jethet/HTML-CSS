My name is August Ada King. I'm the Countess of Lovelace.

I am a mathematician and a writer. People know me from my work on Charles Babbage's early mechanical general-purpose computer, the Analytical Engine. I wrote the first algorithm intended to be processed by a machine. In other owrds, I am the world's first programmer.
